Why a Blog?

I've been meaning to write more for a while. This site has always been a static page — a CV with links — but I want a place to think through ideas in public.

The goal is to write about topics that come up in my day-to-day work: software systems, type theory, static analysis, and occasionally something further afield.

What to Expect

Posts will be irregular. I'm not committing to a schedule, just to writing when there's something worth saying.

If you have thoughts on anything I post, feel free to reach out via email.
