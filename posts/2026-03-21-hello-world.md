## Why a Blog?

I've been meaning to write more for a while. Up to this point, this site has
been a static page — a CV with links — but I want a place to think through ideas
in public.

My goal is to write about whatever comes to me and piques my interest. I expect
that my first few posts will be a bit rough, and that's okay. I am being
intentional about cultivating high follow-through; I am not letting perfect be
the enemy of good.

## What to Expect

Posts will be irregular. I'm not committing to a schedule, just to writing when
there's something worth saying.

If you have thoughts on anything I post, or just want to say hi, please reach
out via [email](mailto:s.xifaras999@gmail.com).
